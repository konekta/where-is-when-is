    About the project

    This project attempts to make use of the plethora of 'data' out there and make it easy to access and use.

    Goal

    Improving access to community services.

    How we do this

    Collection of locations and events regarding community services

    Location upload and publication to an open database.

    Publication of a mobile web service

    We meet the need of the mother looking for a health clinic or school by publishing the data to a mobile client.

    Objectives

    • Deliver services to users on the ground.
    • Build relationships with organisations delivering community services on the ground
    • Definition of users and needs
    • Establish a dedicated open data repository focused on community services.
    • Identification and curation of useful open data
    • Publication of valuable timely open data
    • Engage open source developers
    • Engage data journalists
    • Establish a business model that powers the development of open data
